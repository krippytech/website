Why KrippyTech exists

Built from real IT work.

KrippyTech was created to turn years of practical Microsoft, infrastructure, MSP, and support experience into useful training, tools, and documentation. The goal is simple: help people solve real problems faster and understand what they are doing.

The problem

Too much technical training is either too broad, too slow, too theoretical, or disconnected from the work technicians actually do.

01

Technicians need answers quickly

When a user cannot sign in, a scanner stops working, or OneDrive refuses to sync, nobody wants a forty-minute lecture before the first useful step.

02

Small businesses need practical guidance

Many organizations have someone handling routine IT work because no dedicated administrator is available. Clear, safe procedures can reduce unnecessary support costs.

03

Certification material needs context

Exam objectives become more useful when they are connected to actual environments, customer needs, troubleshooting, security, cost, and long-term support.

The KrippyTech approach

Practical first. Clear enough to use under pressure. Detailed enough to teach something that lasts.

A

Start with the real task

Lessons begin with what the technician or business is trying to accomplish—not with product marketing or theory.

B

Teach the working method

Steps are based on real administrative and troubleshooting workflows, with explanations of why each important step matters.

C

Turn experience into systems

Tutorials, scripts, labs, ticket walkthroughs, checklists, and templates preserve useful methods so they can be reused.
